Introduction

I am fascinated by the dialogue/interplay between art and design, how the former is about asking questions, and the latter about providing answers/solutions.

My background is in film. I have written and directed award-winning films for the big screen. Now my work is focused on designing for desktop and handheld screens. So while my past experience could be said to have been about asking questions of an audience, my present and future about seeking to provide creative solutions for end users.

The art of development and design is in asking the right questions to discover the best solution.

As a recent graduate with a First Class Honours Masters Degree in Applied Digital Media, I am actively seeking to further my career in digital media, UX/UI, web development and design. I have learnt to appreciate that the best designed and delivered products and services result from a deep understanding of the needs and behaviours of the people who use them. Added to my background as an award-winning filmmaker my experiences, training and education have enabled me to develop broad, complimentary, transferable and valuable skill set.
